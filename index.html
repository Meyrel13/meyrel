STITCH

He’s a small, blue, alien experiment who’s designed
to be a destructive force but ends up finding a place in the heart of a
young girl named Lilo and her older sister Nani. Despite his initial mischief,
Stitch’s character evolves into a loyal friend and family member, showcasing
themes of belonging and redemption. His unique appearance, with large ears and
a mischievous smile, along with his endearing qualities, have made him a beloved
character in the Disney lineup.
